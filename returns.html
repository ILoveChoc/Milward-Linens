Milward Linens Returns Policy

In addition to your Statutory Rights, the Milward Linens Returns Policy sets out how you can return your purchase from a warehouse sale because you:

  • Changed your mind; or
  • Purchased an incorrect size and the product doesn't fit

You can return your purchase for an exchange or refund under this Returns Policy if:

  • The product is returned within the 30 day returns timeframe
  • You return the product in its original condition, unused and with the tags attached if the tags were provided by Milward Linens
  • You supply Milward Linens receipt/tax invoice or online order confirmation email as proof of purchase
